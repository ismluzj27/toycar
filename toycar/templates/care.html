Toy Car Care Guide

Keep your toy cars in top shape with these easy care tips.

Cleaning Your Toy Cars

  • Use a soft microfiber cloth to remove dust and fingerprints.
  • For stubborn dirt, use a slightly damp cloth with mild soap.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage paint and plastic.

Storage Tips

  • Store toy cars in a cool, dry place to prevent fading.
  • Keep them in display cases to avoid scratches.
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent discoloration.

Handling & Maintenance

  • Handle with clean hands to prevent smudging.
  • Check wheels and axles regularly for dirt or damage.
  • For collectible cars, avoid excessive play to maintain value.
